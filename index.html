June 1  |  Astoria to Tillamook, OR

Where the River Meets the Pacific

Today marked the official beginning. Dipping the rear tire into the Pacific Ocean at Astoria felt surreal after months of staring at spreadsheets and GPX files. The Oregon coast immediately delivered on its promise of dramatic views and relentless rolling hills.

The weather held up beautifully, though a stiff coastal crosswind made the final ten miles into Cape Lookout State Park a true grind. I’ve pitched the tent in the hiker/biker site, and my legs are definitely feeling the reality of what lies ahead.

May 31  |  Pre-Departure

The Weight of the Panniers

Everything is packed. The bike feels like it weighs as much as a small motorcycle. It is amazing how much thought goes into deciding whether to carry an extra pair of socks or save the 40 grams. Tomorrow morning, we roll out.
